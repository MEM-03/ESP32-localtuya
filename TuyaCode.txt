Byte Ranges:

+4-byte: prefix ( 0x00, 0x00, 0x55, 0xAA )
--- header
+4-byte: seq_nm
+4-byte: cmd_id
+4-byte: length ([payload-with-padding]+hmac)
--- payload (encrypted)
+4-byte: padding (PKCS7)
+N-byte: payload (JSON)
--- hmac
+hs-byte: hmac/crc32-size (16 byte for hmac, or 4 byte for crc32)
---
+4-byte: 4 suffix ( 0x00, 0x00, 0xAA, 0x55 )
